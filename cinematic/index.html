LEVER Framework

AI leadership
for the
Education 4.0 era.

Developed by Dr. Marie Martin, Ed.L.D., LEVER is an equity-centered AI framework transforming how K-12 superintendents lead in the age of human-AI partnership.

Learn About LEVER
L
Lead with Moral Purpose
Ground every decision in equity, human dignity, and the long-arc of student success.
E
Envision Collectively
Co-create shared futures through inclusive visioning with all stakeholders.
V
Vitalize Professional Culture
Build the human capacity and psychological safety that lets innovation flourish.
E
Employ Relationships
Leverage trust networks to navigate change and align communities around shared goals.
R
Reflect & Iterate
Use data and structured reflection to continuously improve and adapt.
