Step 02 — Pick
an interaction level.

Same format can ship at three production depths. Level pricing multiplies the format base — a Micro-Learning at Level 3 costs more than a Curriculum at Level 1. We'll tell you the multiplier on the call; what we won't do is bury it.

Level 01

Foundation

Starts at format base price

What it looks like +
  • Branded visuals, charts, and infographics
  • Click-to-reveal interactions, drag-and-drop, hotspots
  • Light stock or generated B-roll video
  • Standard pre/post assessment
  • Voice-over narration (synthetic or human)

Best for: compliance, policy refreshers, knowledge checks.

Level 02

Production

~1.5× the format base

What it looks like +
  • Custom video shoots and avatar presenters (Synthesia / Hume)
  • Walkthrough recordings with clickable response paths
  • Heavy interactive activities, layered scenarios
  • Branched feedback loops on assessments
  • Higher-fidelity audio & mixed-media production

Best for: clinical training, sales enablement, soft-skill development.

Level 03

Simulation

~2.5–3× the format base

What it looks like +
  • First-person 360° immersive Skybox scenes
  • Full game engine builds (Phaser, Unity-lite)
  • Branching narrative + multi-path scenario design
  • Avatar-driven role-play with response evaluation
  • Longitudinal Day-1 → Year-1 sim scaffolding

Best for: workforce-readiness, certification programs, high-stakes practice.
