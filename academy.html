Four delivery
formats.

Every Academy product ships as a complete kit — facilitator-ready, participant-ready, brand-ready. License the format that fits how your learners will actually meet.

01 Blended · Async + Sync

Flipped Classroom Kits

Learners watch, read, and prep on their own. Live time is spent on application, debate, and feedback. The kit handles both halves.

What's in it +
  • Pre-class video lessons (5–15 min, captioned, branded)
  • Reading packet + reflection prompts
  • In-class activity guide for the live session
  • Discussion protocols & group rubric
  • Post-class assessment + answer key

Best for: K-12 districts, higher-ed faculty, corporate L&D running blended delivery.

02 In-person · Live

Instructor-Led Sessions (ILS)

Bring your trainer. We bring everything they need to teach a polished, equity-conscious workshop on day one.

What's in it +
  • Facilitator's guide with timing, prompts, and pivots
  • Branded slide deck (PowerPoint + Google Slides)
  • Participant workbook + handouts
  • Activity prompts & group exercise materials
  • Pre/post assessments + scoring rubric

Best for: on-site PD days, conference sessions, internal workshops, leadership offsites.

03 Virtual · Synchronous

Virtual-Led Sessions (VLS)

Zoom-ready, breakout-ready, engagement-tested. Designed for remote-first delivery — not adapted from in-person at the last minute.

What's in it +
  • Virtual facilitator script with timing & tech cues
  • Branded slide deck optimized for screen-share
  • Breakout activity packet (Miro / Mural / Jamboard templates)
  • Chat prompts & engagement micro-checks
  • Recording-ready segments for async re-use

Best for: distributed teams, hybrid cohorts, statewide professional development.

04 Capability Transfer

Train-the-Trainer Kits

Stop renting our facilitators. Train your own. Every T3 kit is engineered so a competent in-house trainer can deliver our curriculum at the same quality bar.

What's in it +
  • Master facilitator's guide (deep version, with rationale)
  • Participant workbook + handouts
  • Branded presentation decks (in-person + virtual variants)
  • Assessment rubric + scoring calibration guide
  • T3 video walkthroughs — your trainer learns by watching ours
  • Quarterly office hours for licensed trainers

Best for: scaling internal delivery, cross-site consistency, MSO & FQHC workforce trainings.

How licensing
works.

No subscriptions. You purchase a year-edition of a kit and deliver it as much as you want for the year.

Read the licensing and certificate detail +
01

Per-cohort or per-seat

Choose the model that fits how you scale. Per-cohort licensing for predictable delivery; per-seat for individual enrollment.

02

Brand customization included

Every kit ships with your logo, colors, and name in the right places. Heavier customization quoted separately.

03

One-time purchase, annual refresh

Buy a seat pack or cohort license up-front for the year. Marie refreshes content annually — you re-license at a returning-customer rate.

04

Volume tiers available

Quote-driven for 50+ seats or multi-cohort engagements. T3 kits include quarterly office hours for licensed trainers.
